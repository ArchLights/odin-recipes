Italian Chicken

Description

Delicious marinated and grilled chicken with skin crispy enough for a satisfying crunch!

  • 1 x 16oz bottle of Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  1. Mix the dressing, powder, and salt together then coat the chicken breasts. Cover the dish and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Preheat the grill and oil the grate.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade.
  4. Cook the chicken to at least 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
