Grilled Apple Burger

Description

Juicy burger with grilled apple and chilli crisp perfect for outdoor get togethers.

  • 2 red apples
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp plus 2 tsp chili crisp, divided, plus more for serving
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 slices red onion
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1/4 cup mayo
  1. Slice apple
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degree Fahrenheit
  3. Combine ingredients
  4. Grill patties to 160 degrees Fahrenheit at least
  5. Serve with mayo
